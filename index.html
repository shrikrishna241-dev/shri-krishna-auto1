About Us

Precision Engineering Excellence

Shri Krishna Auto is a government-recognized MSME unit specializing in precision machined components for automotive and industrial applications. Our facility in SIDCUL, Rudrapur operates 24/7 with a fleet of 29 advanced machines.

As a participant in the MSME Lean Cluster program, we have implemented world-class manufacturing practices including 5S workplace organization, waste reduction, and continuous improvement methodologies. Combined with our ISO 9001:2015 certification, we deliver zero-defect components to industry leaders.

Our Evolution

The Journey from Trimurti to SKA

2006

The Beginning

Founded as Trimurti Enterprises with a modest setup focused on precision machining components for local markets. MSME registration obtained to formalize operations.

2010-2015

Foundation of Excellence

A decade of relentless precision. Built reputation for zero-defect manufacturing. Expanded clientele to include major automotive OEMs. Established stringent quality protocols.

2017

Birth of SKA

Rebranded as Shri Krishna Auto to reflect automotive specialization. Major facility upgrade at SIDCUL, Rudrapur. ISO 9001:2015 certification achieved.

SKA
2020-2022

MSME Lean Cluster

Selected for MSME Lean Manufacturing Cluster Program by Ministry of MSME. Implemented 5S, Kaizen, and waste reduction methodologies. Transformed operational efficiency.

2024

Precision Redefined

18 years of manufacturing excellence. State-of-the-art machinery fleet including 2024 Centerless Grinder. Trusted partner to Tata Motors, Dabur, and IREPS.
